Arc Shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A bried, intervening film scene or sequences, not specifically tied to the plot, that appears within a film

Prologue

A speech, preface, introduction, or brief scene proceeding the main action or plot of a film; contrast to epilogue
